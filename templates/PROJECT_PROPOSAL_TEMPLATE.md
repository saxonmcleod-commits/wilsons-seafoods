## 1. Executive Summary
[Client Business Name] needs a digital presence that reflects the quality of its brand. This project will deliver a premium, high-performance website with a custom Content Management System (CMS) to empower the team to manage updates easily.

## 2. Scope of Work

### Phase 1: Design & Development
* **Custom UI/UX Design:** A unique, on-brand design.
* **Responsive Development:** Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
* **Tech Stack:** React, TypeScript, Supabase.

### Phase 2: The "Power" Features (CMS)
We will build a custom Admin Panel allowing you to manage:
* **Opening Hours:** Update instantly for holidays/emergencies.
* **Products/Services:** Add photos, descriptions, and prices.
* **Announcements:** Post "Today's Specials" or news banners.

### Phase 3: Launch & SEO
* **SEO Optimization:** Proper meta tags, sitemap, and structure.
* **Performance Tuning:** 90+ Lighthouse score target.
* **Hosting Setup:** Deployment to a global CDN.
