## 2. Managing Your Content

### Updating Opening Hours
Keep your customers informed about when you are open.
1.  Log in to the Admin Panel.
2.  Navigate to the **"Settings"** or **"Hours"** section.
3.  Adjust the times for each day of the week.
4.  Click **"Save Changes"**. The website will update immediately.

### Updating "Today's Catch" / Specials
Showcase your fresh products or daily specials.
1.  Go to the **"Products"** or **"Specials"** tab.
2.  Click on the item you want to edit (or click "Add New").
3.  Update the **Name**, **Price**, and **Description**.
4.  (Optional) Upload a new photo by clicking the image area.
5.  Click **"Save"**.

### Changing Site Images
You can refresh the look of your site by updating the main images.
1.  Go to the **"Images"** section.
2.  Select the area you want to change (e.g., "Hero Background", "About Section").
3.  Upload your new image.
    *   *Recommended Size:* 1920x1080 pixels for full-screen images.
4.  Click **"Save"**.
